Photoshop Training Courses

Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Canberra

Learn Adobe Photoshop in small instructor-led courses or organise your own private training. We share time saving techniques and best practice workflows for creating, editing and optimising images for either print or web. Courses include free refreshers and after course support.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! While we recommend using the latest version - Photoshop CC (Creative Cloud), we also have CS6, CS5 and CS4 as options.
