Digital Publishing Courses

Having trouble working out which digital publishing medium is best for you? Let us help.

Create Interactive PDFs

Turn existing InDesign files into interactive PDFs that can be distributed by email or downloaded from your website. Reduce your print costs for Annual Reports, Catalogues, Company Brochures etc and add interactivity, navigation, video and functionality that helps your readers find the information they want faster. Interactive PDFs are not limited to printed materials - you can also create stylish and professional presentations, adding special effects such as page transitions and animations to emphasise your message.
See full InDesign Interactive PDFs course outline.

Create Apps for Tablets and Smartphones

The world has embraced tablets in a big way. Every business wants a great iPad App but most are unaware that they can build their own using nothing more than InDesign. Like interactive PDFs, you can use your existing InDesign files for magazines, catalogues, learning materials etc to create Apps that can be viewed on Tablets and Smartpones. Apps offer even more interactivity options and features such as image sequences, interactive slide-shows, 360° panoramas etc. Once you’ve created your App, you can then publish in stores like iTunes.
See full Digital Publishing Suite course outline.

Create eBooks for Kindle, Kobo etc...

Are you looking to digitally publish a book? Leverage the layout capabilities of InDesign and easily export out an eBook that will look great on everything from a Kindle to a Nook to an ereader on an iPad. eBooks do have limited layout options and less interactivity, but do allow the inclusion of images, video and audio.
See full InDesign to ePub course outline.
