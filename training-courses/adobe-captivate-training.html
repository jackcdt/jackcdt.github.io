Captivate Essentials Course

After completing City Desktop Training's introductory Captivate course for eLearning, students will know how to use best practices and efficient workflows to create and publish demonstrations, simulations, and assessments that incorporate a rich variety of multimedia content. Our Captivate trainers all work with the application to develop elearning projects for clients and have extensive knowledge and experience that relate to the other areas of successful elearning methods - outside of Captivate itself.

Captivate 8

Captivate 8's new features are worth the upgrade. You can now produce your elearning materials to display on multipe devices with Captivate's all-new responsive authoring. Also, use an intuitive User Interface to transform PowerPoint presentations into engaging eLearning.

Captivate 7 & 6

As many people still are using the earlier versions of Captivate, we have separated courses offered in Captivate 7 and 6. We do, however, highly recommend to upgrade to the latest version due to the many powerful new features available.
