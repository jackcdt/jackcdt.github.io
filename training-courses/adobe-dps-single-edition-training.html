DPS Single App Publishing Course

Did you know that your Creative Cloud subscription entitles you to creating as many iPad apps as you want? Of course you can do more with a DPS Professional license, but fact is you can get your annual report, portfolio, catalogue or other interactive documents online on the Apple App Store after having taken this special course.

We will guide you through all the necessary steps for publishing your folio, creating your page on iTunes and getting your application online.

This course will focus on iPad apps but iPhone & Android app training is also available on request. Please note that after course support can be arranged for your specific publishing requirements.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
