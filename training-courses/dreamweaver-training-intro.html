Dreamweaver Essentials Course

Build, edit and update responsive websites using Dreamweaver, the leading industry-standard web authoring software. From planning the layout to uploading files to testing for multiple devices and browsers, all the essential steps in an efficient comprehensive workflow will be covered.

This is an introductory course, aimed at beginners, but if you are completely new to web development, it is strongly recommended that you attend the 1 Day HTML & Web Principles to gain the necessary foundation.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
