InDesign to eBooks Course

Create eBooks for Kindle and other eReaders using InDesign. This course will cover best work practices, time-saving tips and setting up the ideal document structure. Gain an understanding of re-flowable content and the new features becoming available with ePub3. Learn how to create the required file formats for different devices and how to edit and optimise the output for your target audience.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
