After Effects Advanced Course

With extra features and better workflows regularly being added to Adobe After Effects, learn how to utilise the application to it’s full potential.

Learn about advanced 3D kinetic typography and infographic techniques, using expressions, audio and linking vision to audio, working across multiple compositions. Industry standard tips and tricks for colour correction and special effects are some of the key learnings in this course.

Special emphasis is placed on new Creative Cloud features, which are updated every two months.

Especially with After Effects, we recommend using the latest version - CC 2015 (Creative Cloud). Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe Creative Cloud.

As an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – participants receive upon course completion a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.

Course Outline

Course PDF

Advanced Animation Techniques

  • Refining keyframe interpolation in the graph editor
  • Auto orient along a path
  • Character rigging using advanced parenting
  • Using the pan behind tool to move the anchor point
  • Using null objects
  • Import motion paths
  • Character animation

Advanced 3D

  • Animating the camera with a null object
  • Camera depth of field and material options
  • Creating simple 3d objects and scenes
  • Auto orient layers towards camera
  • Nesting and collapse transformations
  • Ray Traced 3D
  • After Effects/cinema 4D Cineware workflow

Colour Correction

  • Primary and secondary colour correction
  • Colour modes, Luma, chroma
  • Levels, curves, hue/saturation,
  • Three way colour correction with Color Finesse
  • Understanding the histogram, vector scope and RGB parade
  • Change color, leave color effects

Expressions

  • Linking properties
  • Wiggle
  • Simple arithmetic
  • Looping expressions
  • Expression controls
  • Extruding layers with the index expression

Advanced Effects

  • CC-sphere effect
  • Card wipe effect
  • Fractal noise
  • Advanced effects - a News opener

3D Camera Tracker

  • Track video footage
  • Adding cameras and text
  • Creating shadows
