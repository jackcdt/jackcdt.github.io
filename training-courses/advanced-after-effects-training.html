After Effects Advanced Course

This 1 day advanced class is a follow-on course to the After Effects Essentials course, with students learning how to use Adobe After Effects to it’s full potential. Best practices, as well as shortcuts, are emphasised. See the outline for complete details.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - After Effects CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.

Course Outline

Download
Course PDF

Advanced Animation Techniques

  • Refining keyframe interpolation in the graph editor
  • Auto orient along a path
  • Character rigging using advanced parenting
  • Using the pan behind tool to move the anchor point
  • Using null objects
  • Import motion paths
  • Character animation

Advanced 3D

  • Animating the camera with a null object
  • Camera depth of field and material options
  • Creating simple 3d objects and scenes
  • Auto orient layers towards camera
  • Nesting and collapse transformations
  • Ray Traced 3D
  • After Effects/cinema 4D Cineware workflow

Colour Correction

  • Primary and secondary colour correction
  • Colour modes, Luma, chroma
  • Levels, curves, hue/saturation,
  • Three way colour correction with Color Finesse
  • Understanding the histogram, vector scope and RGB parade
  • Change color, leave color effects

Roto Brush

  • Using the Roto brush
  • Refining the matte

Expressions

  • Linking properties
  • Wiggle
  • Simple arithmetic
  • Looping expressions
  • Expression controls
  • Extruding layers with the index expression

Advanced Effects

  • CC-sphere effect
  • Card wipe effect
  • Fractal noise
  • Advanced effects - a News opener

3D Camera Tracker

  • Track video footage
  • Adding cameras and text
  • Creating shadows
