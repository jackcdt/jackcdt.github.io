Illustrator Advanced Course

Design is a competitive industry and design software advances at a rapid rate to help designers evolve their creativity and stand out among the crowd. If you are a confident, regular user of Adobe Illustrator who is wanting to create eye catching special effects or prepare illustrations to take into Flash or Edge to animate, then our Illustrator Advanced course is perfect for you.

Aimed at participants who have a strong understanding and application of the program, this two day course is packed with information to help you create special effects, work with 3D and perspective grids and advance your rendering techniques. In addition, you will get to learn great tips and tricks on combining Illustrator creatively with other Adobe programs. Push your creativity to the next level.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - Illustrator CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion particpants recevie a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
