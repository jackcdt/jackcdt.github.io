SEO Essentials Course

Success with Search Engine Rank Positioning (SERPs) is a key element in getting found online and getting your product in front of potential customers. Whilst it’s not *the* only way to get traffic, it’s the first way most people think of when they think “traffic”.

However, there are only 10 spots available on that first page; and the top 3 get the lion’s share of that traffic. Where does your website show up? Position 5? Page 5? How about page 50! If you don’t know what your competitors are doing to get above you in the SERPs you don’t stand a chance.

If you want better results in the search engines then you must be prepared to fight for it, it won’t just happen on its own! The Search Engine Optimisation workshop is a power-packed hands-on program that will guide you through today’s leading marketing strategies showing you how you can rank higher in the search engines.

Course Outline

Workshop Outline

  • Understanding the basic concepts of SEO and how it constantly changes.
  • The ‘Magic Half Dozen’: The ranking factors that make the biggest difference.
  • From the “horses mouth”: What Google SAYS about SEO (big surprise).
  • Myths Vs Reality: Why you shouldn’t listen to what ‘everyone’ says.
  • What you SHOULD do and what you SHOULDN’T do (this is vital).
  • On Page SEO Vs Off Page: 21 key strategies and how to implement them.
  • Hands on Ranking Strategies: Here’s where we put it into action.
  • How to create your own custom traffic plan month by month (we’ll actually be DOING THIS on the day).

Expected Outcomes

  • By the end of this one day program you will have a solid understanding of the major ‘terms’ used in SEO. You’ll also understand which practices are good for your business and which ones are bad.
  • You’ll know how to check your existing website for positive on-page SEO practices and how to apply, monitor and master off-page SEO best practices.
  • If you’re looking to build a new website you’ll have a complete understanding of what your web developer should be doing for you – or even enough knowledge to DO IT YOURSELF if you’re familiar with basic web design.
  • Most importantly you’ll be able to look at and analyse your own website and its current positioning in the SERPs as well as analyse your competition and design a custom traffic strategy to improve your positioning, even dominate the rankings.
