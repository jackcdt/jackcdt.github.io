Online Marketing Business Tools Courses

Everyone knows the power and reach of SEO, Web Analytics and other online marketing theories and tools, but not many businesses are using them to their full potential. Defining a clear and organised approach, and understanding which areas to focus on first, will help your business have a professional and interactive online profile.

City Desktop Training is offering a 1 day social media course that teaches the theory and background on the strategy, tactics, generating content and best practices for setting up your campaigns. We also offer 1 day intensive course focussing specifically on LinkedIn application.
