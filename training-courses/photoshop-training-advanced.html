Photoshop Advanced Course

Photoshop version 1.0 shipped in 1998, and in the years since it has grown and evolved into the industry standard in image editing. As a professional designer, keeping pace with new features and techniques is an essential part of your professional development.

If youâ€™ve been using Photoshop for a while now but would like to develop your imaging skills, our Photoshop Advanced course will give you the boost you need. Emphasis is placed on understanding the process and how to use a combination of functions to develop the effects you want. We explore various approaches to creating special effects, and delve into new features as well as features of the application you may not have known existed. Along with developing your image editing techniques youâ€™ll develop your workflow skills through the use of shortcuts and automation to get your work looking better, faster.

Aimed at participants who have a strong understanding and application of the program, are confident in making selections, using adjustment layers and layer masks, this two day course is packed with real-world techniques and exercises to introduce you to some of the newest features and give you a fresh appreciation of professional techniques that have been around for years.

Prerequisite: Students should have either completed the Photoshop Essentials course or should have been using Adobe Photoshop for several years on a regular basis. A Photoshop assessment is available if you are unsure of your Photoshop user level, please email us at info@cd.com.au
