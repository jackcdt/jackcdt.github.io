InDesign Accessibility Course

This 1/2 day course focuses on using InDesign to create highly accessible PDFs. Such documents are able to be read by assistive technologies that aid people with disabilities including poor vision and limited mobility.

Creating accessible PDFs gives everyone, regardless of their abilities, access to the content in your documents. Australian government compliance now requires that all government website content, including PDFs, be accessible.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - InDesign CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
