Premiere Pro Advanced Course

This one day course expands on knowledge gained in the Premiere Pro Essentials to bring your skills to the next level. Topics include advanced editing techniques, replacement of clips, nesting, linking and markers. Image stabilisation and colour correction effects are covered in detail, along with advanced audio techniques and dynamic linking to programs like After Effects, Speedgrade and Audition to achieve high-level industry outcomes. You will also learn best practices for optimising your workflows and file management

As part of the Creative Cloud workflow, new features, changes, and enhanced functionality to Premiere CC are covered.

This one day course will fully equip you to harness advanced features of Premiere Pro with industry experts using real world examples.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - Premiere Pro CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion particpants recevie a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.

Course Outline

Download
Course PDF

Advanced Editing

  • Dynamic trimming/JKL trimming
  • Extend edits
  • The Trim Panel
  • Split edits (L and L cuts)
  • Sub clips
  • Replacing clips and retaining attributes
  • Sync lock
  • Nesting sequences

Stabilizing Footage

  • Analyzing footage with the Warp Stabilizer Effect
  • Smoothing motion removing motion
  • Stabilization methods (subspace warp, position, rotation, scale).

Markers

  • Creating clip and track markers
  • Navigating with markers
  • Adding comments

Syncing External Audio

  • Syncing clips by in points
  • Syncing clips automatically by audio
  • Merging Clips
  • Linking clips on the timeline

Multi Camera Editing

  • Syncing clips by markers, in points and by audio
  • Adding clips to a sequence
  • Creating a multicamera target sequence
  • Switching between multiple cameras

Colour Correction

  • Three way colour corrector
  • RGB curves
  • Video scopes: Histogram, Waveform, vectorscope, RGB Parade
  • Primary and secondary color correction
  • Replace color and change to color effects
  • Broadcast safe settings
  • Adjustment layers

Speech to Text Transcription

  • Analysing speech in the Metadata panel
  • Embedding Meta data in Adobe Media Encoder
  • Editing speech meta data
  • Navigating the video clip using metadata
  • Copying Meta data text

Project management

  • Dynamic Link with Adobe After Effects
  • Dynamic Link with Adobe Encore
  • Importing other premiere projects
  • Relinking/replacing media
  • Archiving and consolidating projects
  • Exporting to Final Cut Pro and Avid Media Composer
