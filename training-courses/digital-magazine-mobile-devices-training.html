InDesign to iPad Course

Learn how to use InDesign and the Adobe Digital Publishing Suite to produce interactive publications for tablets and mobile devices. Techniques taught include: building panoramas, using 3D viewers, incorporating video and audio, animation and linking to online content. Ideal for producing eMagazines, online Annual Reports, How-tos and Course Materials etc. This class is taught by one of the few Adobe Certified DPS Instructors in Australia.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.

  • Training Modules (Overview only)

    • Preparation of Assets—best file formats for images, video and audio
    • How to create a Panorama using third party software
    • How to create an image sequence
    • Creating source documents in InDesign
    • Linking to the web
    • Linking to Articles
    • Adding interactive objects
    • Making Folios with Folio Builder
    • Previewing and testing on the Desktop Viewer
    • Uploading to the Digital Publishing Suite
    • Previewing and Testing in Adobe Content Viewer
    • Making Folios available for download
