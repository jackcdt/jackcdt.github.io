Adobe Captivate Training

When it comes to producing elearning projects, Adobe Captivate is an excellent software choice. Captivate enables you to create interactive training materials that include demonstrations, simulations, scenario-based training, interactive tutorials and quizzes without the need for programming or outsourcing the project. While we recommend using the latest version - Captivate 8, we also have Captivate 7 and 6 as options.

Our Captivate trainers are Adobe Certified, so you can be assured of learning more than just the features, but also invaluable tips and advice on your projects.
