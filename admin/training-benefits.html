Why Train With Us

With 25 years experience, City Desktop Training has set the benchmark for providing quality training and courses to the publishing, education, design, marketing and print industries. Not only do we have the most Adobe Certified instructors in Australia, we also offer the best after training support options.

1. Our Trainers

Our trainers are dedicated professionals with current industry experience and qualifications in training and assessment. Their industry experience is continually updated by Adobe and Apple certification exams. Our commitment to ongoing professional development ensures you will always receive the latest in theoretical and practical training.

2. Adobe + Apple Authorised Training Centre

City Desktop Training delivers the highest quality education at a top venue where every student uses state-of-the-art equipment and software. We think every student deserves to learn in a professional environment and should receive top service going from morning arrival to the closing subjects of a course. Adobe and Apple agree with us and that is why we’re proud to be an Adobe and Apple Authorised Training Centre.

3. Free Project Consult

Are you unsure of the type of course that suits your needs? Then send us your projects and tell us your objectives. This way you’re sure to attend the course that’s right for you, or even participate in a custom one-to-one session where we focus on your business and even work with your own projects.

4. Free Course Resit

We know it’s a little bit scary to start applying what you’ve learned when you get back to the office. Or maybe the circumstances only allow you to use your new skills weeks after having done the course. No worries, those who want to brush up their skills can resit their course for free within 6 months. Just send us an email for more info. (Some conditions apply.)

5. After Course Support

Do you have any questions or problems concerning the new techniques you’ve learned during our courses? Just pick up the phone or send us an email and you’ll get the support you need generally within 24 hours. Our students receive up to 12 months of phone and email support from our trainers. So don’t waste your precious time and get in touch.

6. MAC and PC

It doesn’t matter whether you’re a Mac or PC lover, we’ve got you covered. So specify your prefered system when booking your course and it’ll be ready with your name on it when you arrive.(Sydney and Melbourne office at this stage).
