Premiere Elements Course

With Adobe Premiere Elements, making incredible movies is easier than ever. Bring all you video clips and photos together in one convenient place where you can easily find, view and manage them, and then dive right into a full range of creative activities. Create your movies with smart, automated options that save you time and effort. Enhance your stories with professional effects and sound and share movies with friends and family in interactive online experiences, also DVD, Blu-ray, mobile devices and Youtube.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - Premiere Pro CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
