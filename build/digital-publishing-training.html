Adobe Authorised Certificate Courses

The world has embraced tablets in a big way. Every business wants a great iPad App but most are unaware that they can build their own primarily using InDesign.

You can use your existing InDesign files for magazines, catalogues, learning materials etc to create Apps that can be viewed on Tablets and Smartphones. Adding options and features such as image sequences, interactive slide-shows, 360° panoramas etc is easily done. Once you’ve created your App, you can then publish in stores like iTunes.
