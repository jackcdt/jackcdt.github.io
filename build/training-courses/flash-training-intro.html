Flash Essentials Course

This comprehensive introduction to Flash will provide both graphic designers and web developers with the skills needed to create rich internet applications. Students will first learn how to create engaging web banners and then advance to building a Flash based website that includes animation, video and interactivity controlled by ActionScript. City Desktop has one of the very few Adobe Certified Flash instructors Australia wide. While we recommend using the latest version - Flash CC, we also have previous versions available.

To produce animation & interactivity that works on all devices, including iPads & iPhones, see Adobe's new Edge Animate.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
