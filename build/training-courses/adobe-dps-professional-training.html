DPS Pro App Publishing Course

With the Digital Publishing Suite Pro license you can offer a wide array of services. From building in-company single issue or kiosk applications to selling your DPS services as an agency. But it takes a few steps to create your favourite iPad, iPhone and Android applications. This 1 day course is specifically aimed at companies who own a DPS Pro license and want to find out how you can create these applications.

This course will focus on iPad apps but iPhone & Android app training is also available on request. Please note that after course support can be arranged for your specific publishing requirements.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.

    • Managing the DPS account
    • Using the DPS Dashboard
    • Navigating the Apple provisioning portal
    • Using Apple iTunes Connect

    • Tips and analytics
