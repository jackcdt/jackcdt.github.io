InDesign Advanced Course

The powerful and abundant features of Adobe InDesign are often under utilised or even overseen! Learning about features such as Quick Apply for super fast formatting text, images or objects will save most users 20 to 30 minutes a day, a potential annual time saving of 68 hours! Similarly, understanding the power of GREP could help turn a full day or more of editing into a 5 minute job!

Especially with the latest versions – InDesign CC and InDesign CS6, you can more efficiently design and edit page layouts for print and screen, faster and easier than ever. Use the new Adaptive Design Tools to easily repurpose layouts to look great on a variety of pages sizes, orientations, or devices. Other recent InDesign productivity improvements include time-savers such as the split window, Content Collector tools, grayscale preview, easy access to recently used fonts, and more.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - InDesign CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
