Captivate Advanced Course

City Desktop's Advanced Captivate course follows on from the 2 day Essentials Captivate course. It enables current Captivate users to improve their production skills by incorporating higher-end features such as project templates, design templates, custom styles, system variables, advanced actions and scripts.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
