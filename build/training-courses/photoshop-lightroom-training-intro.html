Photoshop Lightroom Course

Create stunning images from your snap shots with this powerful yet easy to use photo editing software.

Designed for both amateur and professional photographers, Photoshop Lightroom allows for the rapid cataloging of images and sharing them with ease on social networks.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - Photoshop Lightroom CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
