Final Cut Pro X Essentials

Learn the latest version of Final Cut Pro, using the official Apple Pro Training courseware in this two day, hands-on comprehensive course.

Using a number of real-world footage examples, learn both the features and capabilities of the application and the practical techniques that you will use daily in your editing projects.

Learn how to utilise the software and receive tips on best practices and workflows required for professional video editing regardless of output.

The course methodology is designed to enable you to produce professional quality editing in Final Cut Pro as quickly as possible, so you can be productive immediately.

Final Cut Pro X and Final Cut Pro 7

All our scheduled courses use the latest version - FCPX - if you wish to learn FCP 7, then custom training can be organised.
