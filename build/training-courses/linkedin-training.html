LinkedIn for Business, Sales, Learning, Staff Recruitment & Professional Development

Learn how to navigate the world’s largest professional networking site, LinkedIn. This wide ranging course will show you how to manage your professional identity online by building a credible LinkedIn profile, target relevant industry groups, connect with other professionals, review users looking at your profile and much more!

This one day LinkedIn course is conducted by an experienced digital marketing expert who will teach you the best ways to get the most from your time and investment with LinkedIn. The course is ideal for those getting started, but will be beneficial for those with some experience, but not getting the full potential from LinkedIn.

This is one of the few hands-on, practical courses offered, so you learn by doing, backed up by questions answered immediately. Our instructor will outline key sections of theory and practice and then give you the opportunity section by section to put the material into practise by building or extending your own online Profile, as well as reaching out to new or existing Connections.

Outcomes

The course is structured to meet your needs whether they be for getting more business, finding staff, promoting yourself or your organisation, or networking with new or old friends.

At the completion of the course, regardless of entry level, you will gain insights and practices that few current users of LinkedIn know. It will include where and how LinkedIn integrates with the various other social media platforms, and with digital marketing overall.
