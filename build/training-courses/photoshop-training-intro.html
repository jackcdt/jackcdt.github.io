Photoshop Essentials Course

Learn to create, edit and apply special effects to images and text that can be prepared for various uses such as print and marketing materials, presentations, websites, elearning and digital apps. With Photoshop you can easily cut out items, change colours and create compositions. This course introduces the techniques used in Adobe Photoshop for photo-retouching, tonal correction, deep etching, image editing, colour painting and montaging. Learn new features available in Photoshop CC and CS6– Photoshop CS4 and CS5 also available.

Students should have a strong working knowledge of either Mac or Windows.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - Photoshop CC (Creative Cloud). Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
