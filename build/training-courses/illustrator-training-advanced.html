Illustrator Advanced Course

Explore pro techniques behind creating infographics, tracing artwork, applying dynamic effects, working with 3D, perspective grids and other great Adobe Illustrator features. Learn from our industry experienced instructors, who share expert-level insights on the most robust Illustrator techniques to help you achieve mastery for yourself.

Topics include Recolor Artwork, transparency, masks, blend modes and techniques for smoothly integrating workflows with other Adobe programs and are covered in detail. Plus tips and techniques for creating designs using the array of powerful drawing tools so you can start creating professional level vector art.

Our advanced Illustrator course highlights the latest Illustrator CC updates, which is packed with powerful new features, from the latest Brush tool enhancements to new ways to package your artwork, as well as new distribution channels.

Prerequisite: Students should have either completed the Illustrator Essentials course or should have been using Adobe Illustrator for several years on a regular basis. An Illustrator assessment is available if you are unsure of your Illustrator user level, please email us at info@cd.com.au.
