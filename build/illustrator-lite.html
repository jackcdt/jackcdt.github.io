Illustrator Lite Course

Our Illustrator Lite course is designed for those who require a basic understanding of this dynamic vector program, but don't need to use all of Illustrator's features. If you simply need to edit existing Adobe Illustrator documents, draw shapes or understand how to manipulate paths and type, this one day course will teach you these skills and give you the confidence to use them effectively. If you need to learn the program more indepth, you can then attend our 2 day Adobe Illustrator Essentials course.

Our instructors are Adobe Certified, industry experienced and passionate about training! We recommend using the latest version - Illustrator CC (Creative Cloud), but we also have CS6 and CS5 options. Not sure which version? See our article on Adobe software versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
