Training Room Locations

Sydney

Level 4, 60 York St Sydney 2000
The Sydney training rooms are convenient to both Town Hall and Wynyard train stations, located between King and Market Streets and close to the QVB. Nearest parking stations are Wilson Parking and Secure Parking.

Melbourne

Level 7, 20 Queen St Melbourne 3000
Located between Flinders Street and Flinders Lane. Nearest parking is Secure Parking at 376 Flinders Street between Queen and Market streets.

Brisbane

Level 1, 320 Adelaide St Brisbane 4000
Centrally located in the CBD and situated near the corner of Wharf Street. There is an exclusive early bird parking rate for Christie Conference Centre guests at Cathedral Square Car Park (enter via Raff or Ann Streets).

Canberra

Level 1, 33-35 Ainslie Pl Canberra City 2601
Close to London Circuit and only a 15 min drive from the airport. Nearby parking is located in the Canberra Centre and at the corner of Northbourne Avenue and London circuit. The Civic bus interchange is also nearby.
GENERAL
Classes are from 9.00am to approximately 4.30pm. It is recommended to arrive around 8.50am. If you are running late for your course be sure to give us a call on 1300 441 891 so we can inform your instructor on your arrival time.
REFRESHMENTS
Refreshments of water, coffee, tea and biscuits are available throughout the day. Lunch usually occurs between 12.30pm and 1.30pm.
