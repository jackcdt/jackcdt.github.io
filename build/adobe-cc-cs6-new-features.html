Adobe CC & CS6 New Features

With an Adobe Creative Cloud subscription, you have immediate access to a dynamic range of software for print, web, animation and even video editing! You also get access to Adobe services such as Behance, Typekit fonts, and free web hosting with Business Catalyst. Now, designers, marketers, educators and publishers are only limited by their knowledge and not by what software is on their computers.

Whilst this course mentions some of the benefits of the Creative Cloud, the course focus is on the new features specifically in InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop. Highlights like the new Touch Type tool and Package command in Illustrator and the Camera Shake Reduction feature in Photoshop for example.

The course is suitable for people who have been using previous versions on a regular basis. If you are upgrading from CS3, CS4 or CS5, we can provide you a list of features that were introduced in those versions.

At City Desktop Training – an Adobe Authorised Training Centre – upon course completion participants receive a Certificate endorsed by Adobe.
